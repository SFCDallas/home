History


OUR JOURNEY:
The Sharon Fellowship Church of Dallas has a rich and inspiring history, beginning with a small group of dedicated individuals seeking to create a vibrant spiritual community in the heart of Mesquite, Texas.
Founding Moments:
Inaugurated on a significant day, Sunday, October 12th, 1997, by the late Rev. K. V. Johnson (NY), our first worship service was held at the home of Bro. Roy Abraham on Wayfaring St., Mesquite. The church's founding members included families like Brothers Roy Abraham, Finny Varughese, Raju John, Joseph T. Joseph, and John M. Varughese.
Early Leadership:
During our initial stages, we didn't have an official pastor, but the spiritual guidance of pastors M. D. Samuel (Punjab, India), Abraham Joseph, and Paul Gopalakrishnan (Kerala, India) helped us conduct services. After three months of services in the house, we ventured into various rental spaces.
Pastoral Leadership:
In July of 1998, an important chapter unfolded as Rev. Mathews Daniel assumed the role of our first full-time pastor, bringing stability, spiritual growth, and pastoral care to our church family.
Embracing Growth:
From the very beginning, we've had a fervent focus on the spiritual development of our younger generation. We launched Sunday School and youth programs simultaneously with the inception of the church.
Impactful Initiatives:
The Sharon Fellowship Church of Dallas pioneered the Sharon Family Conferences in the United States, hosting the first SFCNA family conference in Dallas in September 2000. This endeavor blossomed, leading to subsequent conferences in 2006, 2009, and 2016. In 2013, we also successfully organized the SFCNA Youth Conference.
Building for God's Glory:
In the year 2000, through God's grace, despite our modest numbers, we were blessed to acquire 4.04 acres of land in the heart of Mesquite. This land became the foundation for our beautiful sanctuary, completed in April 2006. The dedication ceremony was a remarkable day in our history, led by Rev. Dr. T. G. Koshy, then president of Sharon Fellowship Churches.
Honoring Our Leaders:
We hold in gratitude the memory of our former pastors, including Pastors Mathews Daniel, Kuruvila Varghese, M. G. Johnson, Shibu Thomas, Viju Joseph, and Sabu George. Their dedication and service have played a vital role in our spiritual growth.
Mission Heart:
From the outset, we've been passionate about mission work, supporting various mission fields in India and locally.
A Growing Family:
Through the years, our church family has grown, and we now have 45 families united in worship. We're excited about the ongoing construction of a 16,000 sq. ft. multi-purpose building on the remaining land adjacent to our sanctuary, all for the glory of our Almighty God.
A Beacon of Hope:
We continue to aspire to be a city upon a hill, radiating God's light, bringing glory to His name. Let's join in prayer, believing that God will continue to use us for His kingdom, empowering us to make a positive impact in the years to come.

Mission & Vision


OUR MISSION

At Sharon Fellowship, Dallas, our mission is to empower individuals of all ages, nurturing their spiritual growth, fostering social connections, and nurturing leadership skills. Our goal is to raise a generation of Ambassadors for the Kingdom of God who are steadfast in their faith, undeterred by the ever-changing world, and committed to transforming it positively.


OUR VISION

We envision a community where everyone, from the youngest to the oldest, feels the warmth of God's love. We aim to be a church that stands as a beacon of faith, inspiring and uplifting those within our congregation and reaching out to those outside, sharing the message of salvation, hope, and love.
