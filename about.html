History


At Sharon Fellowship, Dallas, our mission is to empower individuals of all ages, nurturing their spiritual growth, fostering social connections, and nurturing leadership skills. Our goal is to raise a generation of Ambassadors for the Kingdom of God who are steadfast in their faith, undeterred by the ever-changing world, and committed to transforming it positively.
